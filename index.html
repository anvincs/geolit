NSR Framework

Notification Services & Records - Our Flagship Platform

A Platform for Private Phygital Marketplaces

Phygital = Physical + Digital

Integrating physical interactions with digital infrastructure for sectors like healthcare, education, maintenance, and consultancy. The NSR Framework enables organizations to create comprehensive digital twins of their operations and build private marketplaces for their services.

1

Digital Twin Creator (Web Portal)

Target Users: System Administrators / Organization Owners

Purpose: Digitally model existing physical organizations - capturing entities like people, services, and communication flows.

  • Notification Inbox: Receive and view updates, reminders, and alerts
  • Interactive Responses: Confirm appointments, request rescheduling, give feedback
  • Service Access: View upcoming/past services, session links (if virtual)
  • Billing and Records: Access invoices, payment history, and view/download service records or reports
2

Service Manager Dashboard (Web Portal)

Target Users: Professionals (e.g., doctors, engineers, teachers)

Purpose: Help professionals manage customer interactions, schedules, and service logs efficiently.

  • Customer Record Management: Access customer profiles, service history, and documents
  • Recurring Notification Support: Setup automated reminders or check-ins (e.g., post-surgery follow-ups)
  • Time Optimization Tools: Appointment scheduling with smart suggestions for availability
  • Efficiency Features: Avoid overlaps and minimize idle time
3

Customer Mobile Application

Target Users: Service recipients and end-users

Purpose: Enable customers to engage with services and manage their interactions seamlessly.

  • Notification Inbox: Receive and view updates, reminders, and alerts
  • Interactive Responses: Confirm appointments, request rescheduling, give feedback
  • Service Access: View upcoming/past services, session links (if virtual)
  • Billing and Records: Access invoices, payment history, and view/download service records or reports
4

Marketplace Platform (Web)

Target Users: External customers and evaluators

Purpose: Showcase and allow transactions for curated service bundles combining people, services, and digital records.

  • Service Bundling: Bundle includes notifications (e.g., welcome + follow-up), services (e.g., consultation + review), and records (e.g., report access)
  • Marketplace Interaction: Discover, analyze, compare, and purchase bundles
  • Flexible Pricing: Support for subscription or one-time purchases
